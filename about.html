Angel's Hobbies & Interests


Dance is a great form of feeling free and being happy, but it is also a social activity. When you dance with friends, you get to know them better and have a good time together.
Dance classes are a great way to meet new people and make friends. When you meet these people regularly, make progress together and have a lot of fun, your relationship will definitely improve.
Also, dancing is a great way to bond with family members. So, if you are looking for a fun way to socialize with friends and family members, try dancing!



Singing can lower stress, improve mental alertness, and functions as a natural anti-depressant, “When you sing, musical vibrations move through you, altering your physical and emotional landscape.
Group singing, for those who have done it, is the most exhilarating and transformative of all. It takes something incredibly intimate, a sound that begins inside you, and shares it with a roomful of people and it comes back as something even more thrilling: harmony” (Horn 1).
