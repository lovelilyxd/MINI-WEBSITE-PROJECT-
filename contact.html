Lovely's Favorite Place



Tubungan

Tubungan, located in Ilo-Ilo province is Lovely's favorite place. Tubungan is a municipality of mostly rolling hills and mountains with very limited plains.
It is located at the southwestern portion of Iloilo province, more or less 42 kilometers from Iloilo City.



Angel's Favorite Place




Kawasan Falls

Kawasan falls is one Angel's favorite place. Kawasan Falls, located on the beautiful island of Cebu in the Philippines have become one of
the most popular attractions in the entire country to visit. In many ways, this rise in popularity is thanks to Instagram.
These twinkly turquoise waterfalls are showcased on nearly every Instagram page or travel blog of anyone who has been to the Philippines.
